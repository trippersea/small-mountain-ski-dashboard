How the score works

How the score works

Every mountain gets a Ski Score that updates daily. It weighs tomorrow's snowfall forecast, the 7-day snow history, how long it'll take you to get there, how busy it's likely to be, and what you told us matters most.

Prioritize powder and the algorithm chases storms. Prioritize a short drive and your closest mountains rise to the top. The score shifts with your preferences, not the other way around.

No black box. No paid placements. The mountain with the best conditions for your specific situation ranks first.

Who built it

Built by a skier, not a committee

WhereToSkiNext.com was built by someone who spent too many Saturday mornings driving to the wrong mountain. Not because the snow reports were wrong — because there was no tool that put all the signals together and just told you where to go. So we built it.

It covers Epic, Ikon, and Indy pass mountains alongside independent resorts, because the best mountain for your weekend might not be the one on your pass.

The deal

Free. No account. No catch.

WhereToSkiNext.com is free to use and always will be. No account required, no email to enter, no ads. Just open it, enter your location, and go ski.
