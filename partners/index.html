Sponsorship never influences recommendations

Algorithm integrity

Our scoring engine is completely separate from sponsorship logic. A mountain's rank is determined by snow, drive time, crowds, and fit — never by commercial relationships.

Transparent labeling

Every sponsored placement is visibly labeled "Sponsored" or "Featured Partner." Users always know what's organic and what's paid.

Relevance gates

Sponsored mountains only appear in results they belong in organically. We sell visibility within relevance, not relevance itself.

User-first, always

The product stays free, uncluttered, and trustworthy. If a sponsorship would degrade the user experience, we don't do it.
