Founding Mountain Partner

Custom early partner package

Built for independent and regional mountains that want to be part of WhereToSkiNext while the platform is still growing. We shape placements, timing, geography, and reporting around your goals and what is live on the site today.

Possible partnership elements may include:

  • Featured visibility when your mountain appears organically in relevant searches, with clear partner labeling.
  • Dedicated mountain summary page with current conditions and a direct CTA to your site when available.
  • State or regional visibility where it fits the product and disclosure rules.
  • Compare-page partner labeling where appropriate, without affecting organic rank.
  • Clearly labeled placements only. No influence on organic rankings, ever.
Optional add-on: State page banner at the top of a state listing, where offered. One slot per state when available. Terms discussed case by case.

Integrity

Sponsorship never influences recommendations

Algorithm integrity

Our scoring engine is separate from sponsorship logic. Rank is determined by snow, drive time, crowds, and fit — not commercial relationships.

Transparent labeling

Every sponsored placement is visibly labeled “Sponsored” or “Featured Partner.” Users always know what is organic and what is paid.

Relevance gates

Sponsored mountains only appear in results they belong in organically. We sell visibility within relevance, not relevance itself.

User-first, always

The product stays free, uncluttered, and trustworthy. If a sponsorship would degrade the experience, we do not do it.
