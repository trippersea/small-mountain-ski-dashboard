Common questions

Where should I ski this weekend near me?

Enter your ZIP code or city at the top of the homepage. We score every mountain within your drive window by live snow forecast, crowd outlook, and pass coverage.

Which ski resort has the most snow right now?

The Storm Chaser or Powder Alert section shows the mountains with the highest 3-day snowfall forecast, updated as the forecast changes.

Is it worth skiing without fresh snow?

Often yes. The scoring weighs recent 7-day snowfall and base depth alongside the 3-day forecast. A mountain with 300" on the base often skis better than one chasing a thin 2" system.

How do I find a quieter ski resort on a holiday weekend?

Set Crowd Level to Quiet Slopes in the preferences panel. The scoring deprioritizes major Epic and Ikon resorts over peak weekends and surfaces independent mountains nearby.

Does WhereToSkiNext cover small local ski areas?

Yes — independents are scored the same way as destination resorts. A local hill with great grooming, low crowds, and a $55 ticket can outscore a $250 resort on the wrong weekend.

How accurate is the snow forecast?

Snow forecasts come from Open-Meteo, a high-resolution weather model updated every few hours. We use summit-elevation-adjusted temperatures to distinguish rain-line events from actual snow.
