Help

Common questions

Quick answers. Your compare view above is the source of truth for this trip.

Where should I ski this weekend near me?

Enter your ZIP or city on the homepage. We rank everything in your drive range using snow, crowds, whether your pass covers it, and price.

Which resort has the most snow right now?

Use Powder alert on this page for high forecast totals, or sort by snow in your full rankings table.

Is it worth skiing without fresh snow?

Often yes. We weigh recent snow, base, and the next few days. A deep base can beat chasing a tiny fresh inch.

How do I pick a quieter hill on a holiday?

Set Quiet slopes in preferences. We rank big pass mountains lower on peak days and highlight smaller spots.

Do you cover small local areas?

Yes. Independents use the same scoring as big resorts. A $55 hill with good grooming and light lines can win a crowded weekend.

How accurate is the snow forecast?

We use Open-Meteo, updated often, with summit temps so rain vs snow is less wrong.
