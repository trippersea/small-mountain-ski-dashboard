FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the Epic Pass or Ikon Pass better for Northeast skiing?

Start with where you ski most weekends. Ikon usually wins Vermont and Maine on this map; Epic usually wins New Hampshire. If Stowe is your top priority, Epic is the simple answer. Confirm current resort lists for your pass tier—access changes by season.

Is Stowe on Epic or Ikon?

Stowe is on Epic. If that one mountain drives your season, the pass choice gets easy. Then verify blackout rules and pass product details for your dates.

What Vermont ski resorts are on the Ikon Pass?

Typically Killington, Stratton, Sugarbush, and Pico. That lineup is the core reason Vermont-heavy skiers often choose Ikon—always confirm the current official list.

Does Epic include Okemo?

Yes—Okemo is on Epic here. If Okemo and Mount Snow matter more than Ikon’s VT quartet, Epic can be the better fit.

Do I need Epic or Ikon to ski in Maine?

For Sunday River and Sugarloaf on these two passes, you’re looking at Ikon. Epic doesn’t mirror that Maine pair on this comparison—double-check the latest maps if Maine is a must.

Hunter vs Windham: Epic or Ikon?

Hunter is Epic; Windham is Ikon. Pick the pass that matches the Catskills mountain you’ll actually ski most.
