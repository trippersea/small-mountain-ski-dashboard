Paoli Peaks at a glance

Paoli Peaks sits in Indiana with 300 ft of vertical drop, 18 trails, and 120 skiable acres. Paoli Peaks is well-suited to intermediate skiers, with 50% of trails in that range. Night skiing available. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 25" of snowfall per season with 6,000 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $55; day-of pricing varies. Paoli Peaks is a Indy Pass mountain.
