Snowmass — At a Glance

Snowmass is a ski resort located in Colorado, rising from 8,104 ft to 12,510 ft with 4,406 ft of vertical drop. The mountain offers 94 trails across 3,132 skiable acres — 6% beginner, 51% intermediate, and 43% advanced terrain.

With an average of 300" of snowfall per season and 430 GPM of snowmaking capacity, Snowmass can typically maintain good coverage throughout the winter. The mountain features a terrain park.

Snowmass is a Ikon Pass mountain. Day tickets start around $299 — prices vary by date and demand. For live snow conditions, drive-time estimates from your location, and a personalized Ski Score, use the SkiDecision app.
