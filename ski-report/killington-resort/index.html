Killington at a glance

Killington sits in Vermont with 3,050 ft of vertical drop, 99 trails, and 1,830 skiable acres. Killington is well-suited to intermediate skiers, with 33% of trails in that range. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 292" of snowfall per season with 50,000 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $199; day-of pricing varies. Killington is a Ikon Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
