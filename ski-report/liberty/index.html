Liberty Mountain at a glance

Liberty Mountain sits in Pennsylvania with 620 ft of vertical drop, 30 trails, and 114 skiable acres. Liberty Mountain is welcoming to all levels with 35% beginner terrain. Night skiing available.

The mountain averages 90" of snowfall per season with 10,000 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $124; day-of pricing varies. Liberty Mountain is an Epic Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
