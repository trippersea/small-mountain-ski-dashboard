Middlebury Snow Bowl at a glance

Middlebury Snow Bowl sits in Vermont with 1,020 ft of vertical drop, 17 trails, and 100 skiable acres. Middlebury Snow Bowl skews toward experienced skiers. About 38% of the mountain is advanced terrain.

The mountain averages 150" of snowfall per season with 80 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $69; day-of pricing varies. Middlebury Snow Bowl is a Indy Pass mountain.
