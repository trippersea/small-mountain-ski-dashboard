Palisades Tahoe at a glance

Palisades Tahoe sits in California with 2,850 ft of vertical drop, 270 trails, and 6,000 skiable acres. Palisades Tahoe skews toward experienced skiers. About 41% of the mountain is advanced terrain. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 450" of snowfall per season with 600 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $240; day-of pricing varies. Palisades Tahoe is a Ikon Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
