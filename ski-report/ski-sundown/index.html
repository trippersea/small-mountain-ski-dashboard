Ski Sundown at a glance

Ski Sundown strips it down: ticket, chair, snow. 30 trails and lift tickets near $79, which is the whole point. Annual snowfall sits around 70 inches (built around guns and grooming more than storms), and snowmaking at 7,000 GPM fills gaps when storms miss. On weekends it the kind of hill where you recognize faces by February. Lift tickets run about $79 at the window.

Best for
  • beginners and learning skiers
  • budget-conscious skiers
  • after-work or night skiing sessions
Not ideal for
  • natural snow seekers. Snowmaking does most of the work
When to go

January and February is peak season. December is hit or miss. The base needs to build first. Weekdays are noticeably quieter if you can swing it.
