Mont Ripley at a glance

Mont Ripley sits in Michigan with 440 ft of vertical drop, 24 trails, and 112 skiable acres. Mont Ripley is well-suited to intermediate skiers, with 45% of trails in that range. Night skiing available. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 200" of snowfall per season with 5,000 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $40; day-of pricing varies. Mont Ripley is an Indy Pass mountain.
