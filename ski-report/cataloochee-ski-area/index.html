Cataloochee — At a Glance

Cataloochee sits in North Carolina with 740 ft of vertical drop, 16 trails, and 50 skiable acres. Cataloochee is well-suited to intermediate skiers, with 50% of trails in that range. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 65" of snowfall per season backed by 4,500 GPM of snowmaking. Lift tickets start around $65 — day-of pricing varies. Cataloochee is a Indy Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update daily. Check back before you go — a fresh forecast can change everything.
