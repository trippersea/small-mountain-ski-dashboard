King Pine at a glance

King Pine sits in New Hampshire with 350 ft of vertical drop, 22 trails, and 44 skiable acres. King Pine is welcoming to all levels with 44% beginner terrain. Night skiing available.

The mountain averages 150" of snowfall per season with 4,500 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $70; day-of pricing varies. King Pine is an Indy Pass mountain.
