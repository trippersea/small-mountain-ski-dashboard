Buttermilk at a glance

Buttermilk sits in Colorado with 2,030 ft of vertical drop, 44 trails, and 470 skiable acres. Buttermilk is welcoming to all levels with 35% beginner terrain. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 200" of snowfall per season with 150 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $249; day-of pricing varies. Buttermilk is a Ikon Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
