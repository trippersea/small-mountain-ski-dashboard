Winter Park at a glance

Winter Park sits in Colorado with 3,060 ft of vertical drop, 166 trails, and 3,081 skiable acres. Winter Park skews toward experienced skiers. About 47% of the mountain is advanced terrain. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 327" of snowfall per season with 400 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $215; day-of pricing varies. Winter Park is a Ikon Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
