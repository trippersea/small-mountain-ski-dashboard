Arizona Snowbowl at a glance

Arizona Snowbowl sits in Arizona with 2,300 ft of vertical drop, 55 trails, and 777 skiable acres. Arizona Snowbowl skews toward experienced skiers. About 40% of the mountain is advanced terrain. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 260" of snowfall per season with 200 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $109; day-of pricing varies. Arizona Snowbowl is a Indy Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
