Deer Valley at a glance

Deer Valley sits in Utah with 3,000 ft of vertical drop, 202 trails, and 4,300 skiable acres. Deer Valley skews toward experienced skiers. About 35% of the mountain is advanced terrain.

The mountain averages 300" of snowfall per season with 500 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $285; day-of pricing varies. Deer Valley is a Ikon Pass mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
