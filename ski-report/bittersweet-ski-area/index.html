Bittersweet at a glance

Bittersweet sits in Michigan with 350 ft of vertical drop, 20 trails, and 100 skiable acres. Bittersweet is well-suited to intermediate skiers, with 40% of trails in that range. Night skiing available. Terrain park on site.

The mountain averages 60" of snowfall per season with 6,000 GPM of snowmaking capacity. Lift tickets start around $45; day-of pricing varies. Bittersweet is a Independent mountain.

Snow conditions update often. Check back before you go. A fresh forecast can change everything.
