Oak Mountain — At a Glance

Oak Mountain is a ski resort located in New York, rising from 1,750 ft to 2,400 ft with 650 ft of vertical drop. The mountain offers 31 trails across 130 skiable acres — 45% beginner, 27% intermediate, and 18% advanced terrain.

With an average of 160" of snowfall per season and 1,800 GPM of snowmaking capacity, Oak Mountain can typically maintain good coverage throughout the winter.

Oak Mountain is a Independent mountain. Day tickets start around $77 — prices vary by date and demand. For live snow conditions, drive-time estimates from your location, and a personalized Ski Score, use the SkiDecision app.
