How New Hampshire stacks up

New Hampshire packs strong New England variety across Epic, Ikon, Indy, and independent areas. Cannon, Wildcat, Waterville, Bretton Woods, and Loon are standouts. The state works well for weekend trips and day missions from Boston and southern New England.
