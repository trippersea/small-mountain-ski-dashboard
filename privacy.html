Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

WhereToSkiNext.com doesn't sell your data, doesn't require an account, and stores as little as possible — most of it never leaves your browser.

Last updated: June 2025

The short version

The short version

We don't collect your name, email, or any account information — we don't have accounts. If you type in a location, it's used only to calculate drive times and is optionally saved in your own browser, not on our servers. We use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used in aggregate. That's the whole picture.

What we collect

What we collect and why

Data How it's used Where it's stored
Location (ZIP or city) Converted to coordinates to calculate drive times and rank nearby mountains. Never logged or stored on our servers. Your browser only. Saved to localStorage if you check "Remember this location" — you can clear it any time.
GPS coordinates (optional, if you click "Use my location") Same as above — used only to compute drive distances. Requires your explicit browser permission. Your browser session only. Saved to localStorage only if you check "Remember this location".
Filter preferences (pass, snow priority, mountain size, etc.) Restores your settings on your next visit for convenience. Your browser's localStorage only. Keys: ski-planner-weights, ski-pass-pref, ski-ski-days.
Weather & snowfall data Cached to avoid redundant API calls and speed up the app. Your browser's sessionStorage only. Expires when you close the tab.
Page analytics Aggregate usage data (pages visited, buttons clicked) to understand how the site is used and improve it. Google Analytics servers. See Google's privacy policy below.

We do not collect your name, email address, payment information, or any account credentials. There are no accounts.

Note on location data and OSRM: When you enter a ZIP code or city, your coordinates are sent to OSRM to calculate drive times. These requests go from your browser directly to OSRM's public routing server. We do not proxy, log, or store those requests on our end.

Cookies & storage

Cookies and browser storage

WhereToSkiNext.com itself does not set cookies. Google Analytics sets its own cookies (_ga, _gid, etc.) to distinguish unique visitors and sessions — this is standard for Google Analytics.

WhereToSkiNext.com stores the following data in your browser's local storage (persists between visits) and session storage (cleared when you close the tab):

Key Type What it stores
ski-saved-origin localStorage Your saved location (only if you checked "Remember this location")
ski-planner-weights localStorage Your snow, size, value, and crowd priority settings
ski-pass-pref localStorage Your selected pass preference (Epic, Ikon, etc.)
ski-ski-days localStorage Your typical ski days per season setting
ski-wx-cache sessionStorage Cached weather forecast data for the current session
ski-hist-cache sessionStorage Cached historical snowfall data for the current session

You can clear all of this at any time by clearing your browser's site data for wheretoskinext.com, or by resetting filters in the app.

Shareable links

Shareable URLs

When you click the "Share" button or share a resort report URL, your current filter settings — including your approximate coordinates and location label — are encoded into the URL. Anyone with that link will be able to see those settings.

If you'd prefer not to share your location, clear the location field before copying a share link.

Children

Children's privacy

WhereToSkiNext.com is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because we don't collect personal information from any user, there is nothing to delete — but if you have concerns, contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy
